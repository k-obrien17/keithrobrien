## A section heading

Use `##` for sections, `###` for sub-sections. Reserve `#` for nothing: the
title comes from frontmatter, not the body.

Plain prose between headings. **Bold** for emphasis, *italics* when you mean it,
and [inline links](https://www.totalemphasis.com) that point somewhere.
